BESPOKE | MADE TO ORDER

Horllardfashion is a bespoke fashion studio dedicated to creating refined, high-quality, custom-made garments that celebrate individuality and timeless style. Each piece is thoughtfully designed to reflect the personality and vision of the client, combining creativity with precise craftsmanship

With a strong focus on detail, structure, and fit, we create garments that not only look elegant but feel effortless to wear. From bridal to occasion wear and statement pieces, every design is crafted to deliver a perfect fit and a lasting impression

Each garment is tailored to enhance individuality, blending creativity with precision to produce pieces that feel as exceptional as they look. Our process is centred around understanding your vision and bringing it to life with care, artistry, and a commitment to excellence. We are dedicated to delivering a personalised experience where every client feels seen, understood, and confident in what they wear.

Olaide Oladunmoye

Founder & Head Designer

Olaide is a passionate fashion designer dedicated to creating timeless, elegant pieces that celebrate individuality and culture. Her work is rooted in craftsmanship, attention to detail, and a deep understanding of how clothing can empower confidence and self-expression.

With a strong eye for design and a commitment to quality, she specialises in custom-made garments that are thoughtfully tailored to each client. Every piece she creates is more than just an outfit, it's an experience, carefully designed to reflect the personality, vision, and story of the wearer.

From bridal couture to statement occasion wear, she brings creativity and precision together, ensuring that each design feels both unique and unforgettable. Her approach blends modern aesthetics with cultural influences, resulting in pieces that are both contemporary and meaningful.

She believes fashion should not only look beautiful but also feel personal. That's why she works closely with her clients, guiding them through every step of the process to bring their vision to life.
